Introduction to Indian Culture

India is known for its rich cultural heritage, diversity, and traditions that have been passed down through generations.

Festivals

Indian festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Eid are celebrated with great enthusiasm and are an integral part of Indian culture.

Traditions

Various traditions such as weddings, religious ceremonies, and rituals play a significant role in Indian society.

Cuisine

Indian cuisine is diverse and known for its use of spices and flavors. Popular dishes include biryani, curry, and dosa.
